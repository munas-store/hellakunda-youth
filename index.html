About HKYDA

Hella Kunda Youth Development Association (HKYDA) is a community-based youth group founded in 2015 by young people of Hella Kunda, Upper River Region, The Gambia.

We believe development starts with us, the youth. Instead of waiting for others, we contribute our own resources, time and ideas to build the future we want for our village.

Our Vision

A united, peaceful and developed Hella Kunda where youth are empowered and all community members live with dignity.

Our Mission

To mobilise youth and friends of Hella Kunda to contribute monthly, plan and implement community projects, and promote education, health and social development in the village.

Our Core Values

  • Unity
  • Transparency
  • Service
  • Respect
  • Accountability
HKYDA is non-political and non-profit. All contributions go towards community development and supporting members in need.
