When you visit this website, certain technical data may be collected automatically, including:

This website uses Google Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to help us understand website traffic and usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your interaction with this website.

5. Cookies

This website uses cookies to improve functionality and analyze usage. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Contact Forms & Emails

If you contact us by email, we will store your message and contact details for the purpose of responding. We will never share this information with third parties.

7. Data Retention

Analytics data is stored by Google for up to 14 months unless manually deleted. Email correspondence is stored only as long as necessary.
